Let me point out- After

Digital Drawing
2022
Description: "Let me point out" is a two part series of digital drawings that is a take on face maps. We are all able to point out each and every flaw on our faces as if it was mapped out. The before drawing is meant to show how I use to look at myself, whereas the after drawing shows my own personal growth and how I no longer see my flaws as something negative.
